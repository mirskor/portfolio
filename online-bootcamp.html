Online UX Bootcamp

Learning Adobe XD

The course has several pros and cons. The instructor provides a lot of information on how to use the Adobe XD. The course also explains the capabilities of the software and its limitations where other software such as Illustrator and Photoshop can be used. The instructor also goes over the general process of UX design such as product development, user personas, product design, user testing, and deployment. However, the instructor does not really talk about UX itself.

Learning Adobe XD

The course primarily focuses on the design but the course does not explain the “why” of its designs. The course should really be called UI design instead as it seems to be the main focus. So, I would really only recommend if you want to improve or learn your Adobe XD skills.
